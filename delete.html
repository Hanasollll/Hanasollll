Data Deletion Request

Connect Flow  ·  Last updated: June 6, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of the data associated with you in the mobile game Connect Flow (com.supremepotato.connectflow), published by Supreme Potato.

Connect Flow has no user accounts and no login. We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or precise location. The only data tied to you is a randomly generated, non-personal analytics ID (a GUID) and your device’s advertising identifier, used for analytics, crash reporting, and advertising. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How to Request Deletion

Follow these steps to ask us to delete the data linked to your analytics ID:

  1. Open Connect Flow on your device.
  2. Tap the Settings button (top-right of the home screen), then tap “Write us”. This opens an email with your unique user ID already filled in — this ID is what lets us locate your records.
  3. In the email subject or body, write “Data deletion request” and send it to us.

If you can no longer open the Game, you may email us directly at cjs5wo5@gmail.com with the subject “Data deletion request”. Include your user ID if you still have it; without it we may be unable to identify the data that belongs to you, because it is not linked to your name or any account.

We will process valid deletion requests within 30 days and confirm by email once completed.

What Gets Deleted

Data typeAction
Randomly generated analytics ID (GUID) and associated gameplay/usage events (Firebase Analytics) Deleted
Crash logs and diagnostic data (Firebase Crashlytics) Deleted
Advertising identifier and ad-interaction data (Google AdMob) Deletion requested from the provider; you can also reset or remove this ID in your device settings at any time
Level progress and settings stored on your device (local storage) Never uploaded to us; removed instantly when you uninstall the Game or clear its data

Data Retention

Once a deletion request is completed, the analytics and crash data tied to your identifier are removed and not retained further. We may keep a minimal record of the request itself (for example, the date and confirmation) where required to demonstrate compliance with our legal obligations. Aggregated or anonymized statistics that can no longer be linked to you may be retained. Backups and third-party provider systems may take up to 90 days to fully purge the data after deletion.
