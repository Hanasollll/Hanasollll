Privacy Policy

Connect Flow  ·  Last updated: June 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Supreme Potato (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the mobile game Connect Flow (com.supremepotato.connectflow) (the “Game”) on Android and iOS.

For the purposes of the EU/UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), Supreme Potato is the data controller responsible for your personal data described in this policy. You can reach us using the contact details in Section 11.

Connect Flow is a free, ad-supported puzzle game. It has no user accounts, no login, no in-app purchases, and no chat or social features. We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, or precise location. The data described below is collected automatically by us and by trusted third-party services to keep the Game running, fix problems, and show ads.

1. Information We Collect

Information stored only on your device

Your level progress and settings (current level, language, sound/vibration toggles) are saved locally on your device using the operating system’s local storage. This data stays on your device, is not uploaded to us, and is removed if you uninstall the Game.

The randomly generated analytics ID is not linked to your real-world identity. It exists so we can understand aggregated gameplay and respond to support or data requests you send us by email.

2. How We Use Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Game and its levels.
  • To understand how players progress through levels (aggregated analytics).
  • To detect, diagnose, and fix crashes and performance issues.
  • To display advertisements, including personalized ads where permitted.
  • To respond to support inquiries and privacy requests you send us.

3. Advertising

Connect Flow displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob and its partners may use your advertising identifier and the data above to deliver and measure ads, including personalized advertising.

  • You can reset your advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads at any time in your device settings (Android: Settings → Google → Ads; iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking / Apple Advertising).
  • If you are in the EEA or the UK, we collect your consent for personalized advertising and related data processing through a Google-certified consent management platform before such ads are shown. You can change or withdraw your consent at any time from the “Manage ad consent / Privacy options” entry in the Game’s settings; if you withdraw consent, you may instead be shown non-personalized ads.
  • You can learn more or opt out of interest-based advertising at thenai.org/opt-out and youradchoices.com.

4. Third-Party Services

We rely on the following third-party services, which process data as independent providers under their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase (Analytics & Crashlytics) — Firebase Privacy · Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — How Google uses data · Google Partner Sites

We may also disclose information if required by law, to comply with legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users or the Game.

5. Data Retention

Analytics and advertising data are retained according to the retention settings of the third-party services above. Crash data is retained for a limited diagnostic period. Locally stored progress remains on your device until you clear it or uninstall the Game.

7. Children’s Privacy

Connect Flow is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the European Economic Area), or the minimum age required in your country, whichever is higher. We do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us and we will delete it. Where applicable, ads are configured to comply with policies for general-audience apps.

8. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your data, to object to processing, to data portability, and to opt out of personalized advertising or the “sale” or “sharing” of personal information.

  • EEA / UK (GDPR): access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection, and the right to withdraw consent and to complain to a supervisory authority.
  • California (CCPA/CPRA): the right to know, delete, and correct your personal information, and the right to opt out of the “sale” and “sharing” of personal information. We do not sell your personal information for money. However, our use of Google AdMob for interest-based advertising may be considered “sharing” for cross-context behavioral advertising under the CPRA. To opt out of this sharing, disable personalized ads in your device settings as described in Section 3 — no request to us is required, and we do not discriminate against you for opting out.

Because the data we collect is tied only to a randomly generated identifier and is not linked to your name or account, we may be unable to associate it with a specific individual. To exercise access, deletion, or correction rights, contact us at the address in Section 11; where it helps us locate your records, include the user ID shown in the email pre-filled by the Game’s “Write us” option.

9. Data Security & International Transfers

Data transmitted to our service providers is encrypted in transit. Because we use Google services, your data may be processed on servers located outside your country, including the United States. We limit access to data to what is necessary to operate the Game.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date above. Significant changes may be communicated through the Game or the app store listing. Your continued use of the Game after changes take effect means you accept the updated policy.
